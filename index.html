🎉 December – Celebration aur Thank You

December sirf year end nahi, humare saath ke ek poore saal ka celebration hai.

Tumhare saath bitaya har moment special tha.

Thank you for being the most beautiful part of my life.

Tum meri sabse pyari gift ho.❤️

🌻 November – Bharosa

November ki tarah humara pyaar ab mature ho gaya hai.

Ab bas ek hi cheez hai pura bharosa. Tum ho,

toh sab kuch sahi hai.

Tum meri life ka wo anchor ho jo mujhe kabhi tootne nahi deta.❤️

🌼 October – Sapne

October ki hawa mein jo freshness hoti hai,

waise hi tumne mere sapnon ko ek nayi udaan di.

Ab jo bhi plan banata hoon, usme tum ho.

Tum mera future ho meri planning nahi, meri priority ho.❤️

🌺 September – Khamosh Batein

September ki tarah humari chhup si baatein bhi bohot kuch keh jaati hain.

Kabhi kabhi sirf tumhari aankhon mein dekh kar hi sab kuch samajh aa jaata hai.

Tum samajhne mein best ho bina bole bhi.❤️

🌹 August – Azadi aur Apnapan

August ne yeh samjhaya ki pyaar mein space aur sammaan hona kitna zaroori hai.

Tumhare saath main khud ko express kar sakta hoon bina kisi darr ke.

Tum ho toh sab kuch apna sa lagta hai.❤️

🌸 July – Emotions

July ki baarish jaise dil ko soft kar deti hai,

waise hi tumhare saath mere har emotion ka ek alag meaning hai.

Tumhare saath rona, hansna –

sab kuch asli lagta hai. Tum meri feeling ka perfect jawab ho.❤️

🌼 June – Muskaan

June ke garam dinon mein bhi

ek smile sab kuch thanda kar deti thi.

Tumhari hansi meri duniya ki sabse pyari awaaz hai.

Tumhare saath rehna matlab roz naye khushi ke reason milna.❤️

🌺 May – Saath ka Sukoon

Jaise garmi mein thandi hawa sukoon deti hai,

waise hi tumhara saath mere har stress ko shanti mein badal deta hai.

Tumhare paas hona ek comfort jaisa lagta hai

– safe, warm aur real.❤️

🌻 April – Samajhdari

April mein hum sirf hasi-mazaak nahi, ek doosre ki soch,

feelings aur problems bhi share karne lage.

Tumhari samajhdari ne mujhe ek better person banaya.

Tum bas ek patni or girlfriend nahi, meri best friend ho.❤️

🌼 March – Apnapan

March jaise mausam change hota hai,

waise hi tumne meri feelings ko ek nayi direction di.

umhari presence ne mujhe yeh feel karwaya ki ab main akela nahi hoon

– ab hum dono ek doosre ke liye hain.❤️

🌷 February – Pyaar ka Pehla Rang

February sirf Valentine ka month nahi tha,

yeh hum dono ke pyaar ke izhaar ka waqt tha.

Tumhari har baat mein ek sweetness thi jo dil ko chu jaati thi.

Tum meri sabse special feeling ban gayi ho.❤️

🌸 January – Shuruaat ka Ehsaas

January ki tarah tum meri life mein ek nayi umeed ban kar aayi.

Tumhari muskaan ne meri boring si duniya ko roshan kar diya.

Tumhare saath bitaya har pal ek nayi shuruaat jaisa laga –

, pure aur full of love.❤️
